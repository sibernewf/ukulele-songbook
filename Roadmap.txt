🚀 FUTURE ROADMAP
The following features are planned for future versions.


⭐ v6.2 — Personal Song Ratings
Allow each user to rate how much they enjoy a particular song version.
Example:
★★★★★ Perfect. A favourite I play often.
★★★★☆ Very enjoyable.
★★★☆☆ Average. Keep it in the library.
★★☆☆☆ Rarely played.
★☆☆☆☆ Consider removing.

Possible uses:
 - Sort songs by personal rating
 - Filter to favourite 5-star songs
 - Combine with play statistics
 - Identify favourite versions of duplicate songs
 - Identify songs that may be removed

Stored as personal data, separate from the original song files.


🎼 v6.3 — Song Versions and Alternate Arrangements

Support multiple versions of the same song.

Examples:
Country Roads - Original
Country Roads - Easy Beginner
Country Roads - Fingerstyle
Country Roads - Campfire Version

Possible future library fields:

version: "Easy Beginner"

Benefits:
 - Keep different arrangements of the same song.
 - Compare beginner and advanced versions.
 - Store fingerstyle and chord versions separately.
 - Avoid losing useful arrangements.
